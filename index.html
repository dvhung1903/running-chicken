About Ostrich Run

🦤 What is Ostrich Run?

Ostrich Run is an endless runner where you control a cool ostrich dodging black eggs and stone stalactites. Collect coins, dive into underground tunnels, grab iron armor and beak upgrades — and beat the global leaderboard!

🎮 How to Play

Jump — Tap ↑ or the Jump button. Press Space / Arrow Up on desktop.

Duck — Tap ↓ or the Duck button. Duck twice to enter a tunnel.

Pause — Tap ⏸. The game auto-resumes after 10 seconds.

⚡ Power-ups

🪙 Coins — +3 pts each.

🛡️ Iron Armor — Found in tunnels. Makes you invincible for 10s.

🦴 Iron Beak — Gained after leaving a tunnel with armor. Duck into eggs to smash them for 10s.

🏆 Leaderboard

Scores are saved online. Your best score is updated automatically after each run. Top 10 players are displayed globally.

📈 Difficulty

Speed increases by ×0.05 every 50 points. A warning banner appears 10 points before each speed-up. Tunnels appear from 160 points onward, every 80 points.

👨‍💻 Developer

Built with vanilla HTML5 Canvas, no external libraries. Leaderboard powered by Upstash Redis via Vercel serverless functions.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Ostrich Run collects only the information you voluntarily provide: your chosen display name and your high score. No email address, phone number, or personal identification is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your display name and score are stored on a global leaderboard to allow you to compete with other players. This data is publicly visible to all players of the game.

3. Local Storage

The game uses your browser's localStorage to remember your player name and a local copy of the leaderboard. This data never leaves your device unless submitted to the leaderboard API.

4. Advertising

This game may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdSense or similar providers). These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing activity across websites. We do not control the data collected by these advertising partners.

By playing Ostrich Run, you consent to the display of advertisements. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device or browser settings, or by visiting youronlinechoices.com.

5. Cookies

Ostrich Run itself does not set cookies. However, third-party ad networks embedded in the game may set their own cookies on your device for advertising and analytics purposes.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard data (name + score) is retained indefinitely. You may request removal of your entry by contacting us. Local browser data can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This game is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Third-Party Services

We use Upstash Redis (upstash.com) for leaderboard storage and Vercel (vercel.com) for hosting. These services have their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data removal requests, please contact the developer through the game's distribution platform.
